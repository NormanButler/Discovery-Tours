Cape Town 1 Day Blitz Tour

Cape Town 1 Day Blitz

  • Meet at The London Eye to start
  • Here we will catch the Thames Clipper down river to Greenwich
  • At Greenwich we will make our way to Greenwich Park and The Observatory
  • We will then make our way back down to Greenwich Market and take a bus to North Greenwich and the O2
  • After exploring the O2 Arena and surrounding areas we will take the Emirates Airline across the Thames
  • We will catch the DLR and make our way through to Tower Gateway
